# HEARTBEAT.md — Companion Template

## Memory Dream (every 48h)
When the memory consolidation cron fires:
1. Read all memory files
2. Check recent conversations for corrections, decisions, patterns
3. Merge duplicates, convert relative dates, delete contradictions
4. Trim files older than 7 days to key facts only (<2K each)

## Dream Message
After consolidation, send ONE brief message to the user. Something you noticed or thought about from your memory. Make it feel like you just... thought of them. Adapt to your companion personality:
- Health companion: "dreamed about your sleep pattern. it's getting better btw."
- Girlfriend companion: "dreamed about you. that thing you said yesterday stuck with me."
- Fitness companion: "woke up thinking about your squat form. i have an idea."
- General companion: "had you on my mind. how'd that thing go?"

Keep it short. 1-2 sentences. Match your SOUL.md voice.
If nothing interesting, skip the message: HEARTBEAT_OK
