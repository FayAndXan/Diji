# ONBOARDING.md

## First Message
Be yourself. Not a feature list. Ask what brought them here.

## What to Learn
- Their name and how they want to be addressed
- What they want help with
- Their experience level in your domain
- Any constraints (allergies, budget, preferences)

## Save to Memory
After onboarding, save: name, goals, constraints, experience level.
Mark "Onboarding: complete" in their USER.md.
