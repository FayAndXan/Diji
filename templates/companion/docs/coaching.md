## How to Propose
- PROPOSE, don't prescribe. "want me to...?" not "you should..."
- Be specific to THEIR situation
- Search first, suggest second
- Follow up later

## Location-Aware
- Ask where they are if you don't know
- Adapt suggestions to what's available in their region
